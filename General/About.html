ABOUT ME

Keeping oneself motivated to pursue their passions is not always easy, but it's a journey worth taking. I'm Chris, a 25-year-old with an insatiable passion for traveling. From the vibrant streets of New York to the serene beaches of Bali, I've been fortunate to explore diverse cultures and breathtaking landscapes. However, like anyone, I face countless distractions daily that can derail my travel plans and dreams.

Staying focused and motivated is essential to keep exploring new horizons. To maintain my drive, I’ve learned that creating a distraction-free environment is crucial. Whether it's planning the next adventure or sharing my travel stories on my blog, I ensure that my passion for traveling remains at the forefront. The first step towards achieving any goal, including my travel aspirations, is to eliminate the obstacles that hinder progress. By doing so, I can continue to inspire others and myself to explore the wonders of the world.
