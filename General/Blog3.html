25 Best Things To Do In Niagara Falls, Canada In 2024

By Chris The Traveller

June 17, 2024

1. Visit the Horseshoe Falls

Experience the breathtaking beauty of Horseshoe Falls, the largest of the three falls that make up Niagara Falls. Take a boat tour for an up-close view of this natural wonder.

2. Journey Behind the Falls

Explore tunnels that take you behind the cascading water of Horseshoe Falls. This unique perspective offers an unforgettable experience of the falls' power and majesty.

3. Niagara SkyWheel

Ride the Niagara SkyWheel for stunning aerial views of the falls and the surrounding area. This 175-foot tall Ferris wheel provides a panoramic perspective, especially beautiful at night.

4. Clifton Hill

Stroll down Clifton Hill, known as the "Street of Fun." Enjoy attractions like museums, arcades, restaurants, and themed entertainment for the whole family.

5. Niagara Parks Botanical Gardens

Visit the Niagara Parks Botanical Gardens to see beautifully maintained gardens, including a rose garden with over 2,400 roses. The gardens are also home to the Butterfly Conservatory.
